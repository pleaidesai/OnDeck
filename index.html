The process

The redefined mediator:
coordinating the family journey

The mediation process doesn't end with an amicable agreement — it's ensuring ongoing continuity and alignment as families grow and evolve.

01
Separation and Divorce Mediation
The decision to divorce amicably through mediation itself is a milestone. We begin here, with a holistic focus on children and the entire family, resulting in the final agreement.
02
Network Selection and Experience Design
Our community of support providers bring expertise that encompasses law, financial planning, career and life coaching, family counseling, tutoring/college planning, and more. You can build a team from our marketplace based on your needs, or invite members of your own network.
03
Family Planning, Milestones, Goals
The online platform brings transparency to 2-home schedules, deadlines for family planning and activities, and next step checklists from selected advisors and supporters. You decide what each participant can see within a fully secure online environment.
04
Family Evolution Through the Years
As families grow and mature needs shift. There may be a new blended family, or even more children. Considerations that seemed far away, such as seeing children off to camp or college, are now a reality. The platform provides a visual timeline from the beginning to document and manage shifting family priorities.
