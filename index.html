The process

From individual contributor
to platform owner

We've done this once — turned a single idea into a licensed, scalable platform that eventually sold. Here's how we help you do the same.

01
Qualify & define your marketplace
We run a structured discovery to map your IP, your existing demand, and the buyer–seller dynamic your platform will serve. Output: a Marketplace Configuration Document that drives the entire build.
02
Prototype in weeks, not months
We stand up a working prototype fast so you can recruit real sellers, validate the experience, and show clients something live before a dollar of production code is written.
03
Build the real platform
Your production marketplace runs on owned code — Next.js, Supabase, Stripe. No lock-in, no monthly SaaS fees eating your margin. You own it outright and can take it in-house anytime.
04
Launch, scale, and step back
Onboard your first seller cohort, activate the buyer discovery flow, and let the platform run. Your role becomes curator and brand — not individual contributor.
